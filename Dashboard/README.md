# Dashboard Explanation



The dashboard is structured following a top-down approach to support executive decision making.
At the top, KPIs such as Revenue, MRR, Active subscribers, and Churn Rate provide an immediate overview of business performance.

The middle section focuses on trends over time, allowing stakeholders to undersand how the business is evolving.

Below, that revenue breakdowns by product and customer segment help identify key drivers of performance.

Finally, subscriptions heal metrics and cusomer value metrics provice deeper insight into growth sustainability and customer profitability.

Filters such as region and product were included to allow flexible analysis across different segments.

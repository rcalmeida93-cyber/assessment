# Stakeholder Explanation

## Executive Explanation

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of business performance, focusing on revenue, subscriptions growth, and customer value.
At the top, key metrics such as Revenue, MRR, Active Subscribers, and Churn Rate give a quick snapshot of business health.
The dashboard also show how revenue envolver ver time and wich products or segments are driving performance. Additionally, it highlights subscription trends, includng new and churned subscribers to help undertand retention.


## How churn is calculared

Churn rate represents the percentage of subscribes who stop their subscription during a given period.
Imagine you start the month with 100 subscribers. If 10 of them cancel their subscription during the month, your churn rate is 10%.


## What MRR represents
MRR shows how much revenue we can expect every month from subscriptors. It reflects the stable and recurring parte of business.
